Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, programs, and approach.

What types of organizations does EBEC work with?

EBEC collaborates with a diverse range of organizations including K-12 schools, higher education institutions, government agencies, non-profits, and private sector companies involved in education. Our clients span across six continents and represent both public and private sectors.

How do your consulting services differ from other education consultants?

EBEC uniquely integrates business strategy, economic analysis, and educational expertise. We combine data-driven insights with practical implementation support, ensuring our recommendations are both economically sound and educationally effective. Our team includes former educators, economists, and business strategists.

Are your programs available internationally?

Yes, our programs are designed for a global audience. We offer both online and in-person options, with regional adaptations to address local contexts and challenges. We've successfully delivered programs in over 40 countries and offer multilingual support for key initiatives.

What is the typical duration of a consulting engagement?

Engagements vary based on scope and complexity, typically ranging from 3-month strategic assessments to 2-year implementation partnerships. We begin with a discovery phase to understand your needs and propose a customized engagement timeline and deliverables.
